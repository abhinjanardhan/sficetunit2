Sfi cet unit

The Student Federation of India as a whole represents the foundation of a reformist, democratic and libertarian education framework which will guarantee social equity and scholarly self reliance.It endeavors to accomplish this by joining the whole Indian graduate network towards the reason for a general and public system of schooling guaranteeing the right to education for all. SFI represents the progression of India towards a reforming example of society, guaranteeing social and monetary balance and an existence of pride and success to all.Hence it raises aloft the motto, 'Independence, Democracy, Socialism'. SFI CET Unit, the largest student outfit in Kerala, has been in the forefront of fighting for students right since its inception. The organisation has been in the midst of struggles for graduate rights and a better education system on one hand and fighting philosophical battles on the other, as the development of an engineer is inclined to both. It has conveyed forward the legacy of the reformist graduate engineers of our nation, which has viewed itself as an invisible piece of the more extensive battle for social change.
